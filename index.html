Our Philosophy

The Release Way

Six core pillars that define our approach to lasting recovery.

01

Personalized Treatment

We tailor a treatment plan to help you succeed — and keep you on track with 24/7 staff support, drug testing, and alcohol monitoring.

02

Community of Support

We connect you to a network of alumni, allies, and peers — providing you with an invaluable sense of understanding and support.

03

Chief Medical Officer

Each resident undergoes a comprehensive medical consultation with our in-house doctor. The CMO provides ongoing medication management throughout treatment.

04

Five-Star Facilities

Our high-end, highly structured sober homes feature luxurious amenities including an on-site chef and laundry services, along with yoga and breath work.

05

Comprehensive Family Support

We offer in-person and virtual family support groups, family education days, and family coaching. We help every family member — not just the individual seeking care.

06

Qualified Staff

Our staff is expertly trained to support your recovery journey. Many have lived experience in our programs, uniquely positioning them to help you achieve lasting recovery.

Clinical Treatment

Treatment Programs

Evidence-based outpatient treatment for adults with substance use disorder, addiction, trauma, and co-occurring disorders.

Intensive

Partial Hospitalization (PHP)

Intensive, structured treatment with optional supportive community housing. Designed for clients needing more support than standard outpatient but not 24/7 residential care.

12 Weeks

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

12-week structured curriculum covering emotional regulation, self-awareness, relapse prevention, interpersonal connectedness. Led by licensed clinicians with CMO oversight.

Professionals

Evening IOP

Specifically designed for working professionals. 12 hours of on-site programming weekly with trauma-informed treatment delivery.

Step-Down

Outpatient Program

Step-down program following IOP completion. Includes weekly individual therapy, group therapy, medication management, and family therapy.

Concierge Care

Recovery Services

Evidence-based recovery services to support you at every step of your life-changing journey. We treat the root cause — so you can achieve sustainable recovery.

Intervention

Customized teams assembled based on individual needs, with non-standardized approaches developed after extensive assessment.

Case Management

Assigned case managers providing regular meetings, clinical referrals, recovery goals, and 24/7 crisis support.

Sober Companion

Round-the-clock accountability with carefully matched companions, gradually transitioning from intensive to reduced support.

Safe Transport

Available 365 days a year, 24/7, using trained Release employees nationwide — never contractors.

Sober Coaching

One-on-one sessions with handpicked coaches, community engagement, and front-loaded support tapering as progress emerges.
